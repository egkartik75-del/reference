OUR HERITAGE

Where Nature
Meets Excellence

Nestled in the breathtaking Chanshal Valley of Rohru, District Shimla, Raja Virbhadra Singh College stands as a beacon of higher education in the Himalayas. Established in 1988 with just 16 students, we have grown into a premier co-educational institution affiliated to Himachal Pradesh University and accredited by NAAC with a CGPA of 3.00.

Perched amidst snow-draped mountains, thick pine forests, and the serene Pabbar River, our campus offers a salubrious climate and an inspiring atmosphere for intellectual growth. We blend the rich Pahari traditions with modern education, guided by our motto — "Bringing Transformation through Education".
